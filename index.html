Welcome

We are the official Manchester City Supporter Group for New Mexico.

We are a group of supporters who get together and cheer on the team and are always welcoming new members.

Meetups

We meet at Two Cranes Bistro + Bar located at 901 Rio Grande Blvd NW Suite A192, Albuquerque, NM 87104. It is just south of I-40 and north of Old Town.

Check out our Facebook Page for our most up-to-date meeting times and dates.
